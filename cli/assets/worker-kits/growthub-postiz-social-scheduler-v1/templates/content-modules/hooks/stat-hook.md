# Stat Hook Module

**Use when:** Data-driven content, social proof, industry insights, trend analysis.

---

## Patterns

| Pattern | Example |
|---|---|
| Surprising number | "73% of buyers make their decision before talking to sales." |
| Comparison | "Companies that [do X] grow 2.5x faster than those that don't." |
| Trend line | "Social media ad spend hit $230B in 2025. Here's where it's going." |
| Personal data | "We tested [X] for 90 days. The results surprised us." |
| Gap stat | "Only 12% of companies do [best practice]. The rest are leaving money on the table." |

## Rules

- Use a specific number, not a vague one ("73%" not "most")
- Cite or attribute the stat if public — builds trust
- The stat must be relevant to the audience's daily reality
- Follow the stat with "here's why it matters" or "here's what to do about it"
- Avoid outdated stats — nothing older than 18 months unless it's a foundational benchmark
