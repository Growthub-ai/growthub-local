# Story Hook Module

**Use when:** Behind-the-scenes, case studies, personal lessons, customer stories.

---

## Patterns

| Pattern | Example |
|---|---|
| Time anchor | "Last month, we almost [dramatic event]." |
| Confession | "I used to [old approach]. Then [event changed everything]." |
| Transformation | "6 months ago, [starting state]. Today, [end state]." |
| Behind-the-scenes | "Here's what actually happened when we [did X]." |
| Customer story | "[Customer name] came to us with [problem]. This is what happened." |

## Rules

- Open in media res — start in the middle of the action
- Use concrete details (names, dates, numbers) to build credibility
- The story must lead to a clear takeaway or lesson
- First sentence must hook — no "I want to share a story" preamble
- Keep the setup tight — get to the tension fast
