# Curiosity Hook Module

**Use when:** Educational posts, product reveals, surprising facts, myth-busting.

---

## Patterns

| Pattern | Example |
|---|---|
| Contrarian | "Most people think [X]. They're wrong." |
| Hidden knowledge | "The one thing [audience] never talk about..." |
| Surprising stat | "[Specific number]% of [group] make this mistake." |
| Challenge | "Stop doing [common practice]. Here's why." |
| Promise | "I spent [time] learning [topic]. Here are [N] things I wish I knew." |
| Question | "Why does nobody talk about [specific topic]?" |

## Rules

- Must create an information gap — the reader needs to keep reading to resolve it
- Be specific, not vague ("87% of marketers" not "most people")
- Deliver on the promise in the body — clickbait without payoff kills trust
- Keep under 125 characters for Instagram (before "...more" truncation)
- Keep under 210 characters for LinkedIn (before "...see more" truncation)
