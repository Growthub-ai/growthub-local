# Story Body Module

**Use when:** Case studies, testimonials, behind-the-scenes, personal lessons.

---

## Structure

```text
[Situation — what was happening before (1-2 sentences)]

[Conflict — what went wrong or what challenge appeared]

[Action — what was done about it]

[Result — what happened (specific outcomes, numbers)]

[Lesson — the takeaway for the reader]
```

## Rules

- Follow the SCAR framework: Situation, Conflict, Action, Result
- Use specific details — names, dates, numbers, percentages
- Keep the conflict relatable to the audience
- The result must be concrete ("grew 40%", not "things improved")
- The lesson must be applicable to the reader's situation
- For long stories: break across carousel slides or thread tweets
