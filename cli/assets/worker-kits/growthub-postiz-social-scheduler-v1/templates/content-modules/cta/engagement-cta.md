# Engagement CTA Module

**Use when:** Building community, driving comments, encouraging saves and shares.

---

## Patterns

| Pattern | Example | Platform fit |
|---|---|---|
| Question | "What's your take? Drop it in the comments." | All |
| This-or-that | "[Option A] or [Option B]? Tell me below." | Instagram, LinkedIn |
| Save prompt | "Save this for your next [task]." | Instagram |
| Share prompt | "Share this with someone who needs to hear it." | All |
| Tag prompt | "Tag a founder who's building in public." | Instagram, Twitter |
| Poll | "Agree or disagree? Vote below." | LinkedIn, Twitter |
| Fill-in-the-blank | "The best marketing tool in 2026 is ___." | LinkedIn, Twitter |

## Rules

- One CTA per post — don't ask for a comment AND a share AND a follow
- Match the CTA to the pillar: community posts get engagement CTAs, product posts get conversion CTAs
- Make the ask easy — "yes/no" questions get more comments than open-ended ones
- Save CTAs work best on educational and reference content
- Share CTAs work best on relatable and emotional content
