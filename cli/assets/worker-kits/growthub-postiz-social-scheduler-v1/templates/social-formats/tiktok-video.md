# TikTok Video Format

## Constraints

```yaml
duration: "15s, 60s, 3min, or 10min"
aspect_ratio: "9:16"
caption_max: 2200
hashtag_recommended: 3-5
```

## Structure

### Script/Storyboard

1. **Hook (0-2s)** — Immediate hook. Text overlay or bold opening statement.
2. **Context (2-5s)** — Why this matters or what is about to happen.
3. **Value (5-45s)** — Main content. Tips, story, demo, or trend.
4. **Payoff (last 3-5s)** — Satisfying conclusion or unexpected twist.
5. **CTA (overlay or verbal)** — Follow for more, comment, share.

### Caption
- Short, conversational
- Reference the hook or add extra context
- Hashtags mixed into natural language
- Trending hashtag if relevant

## Best Practices

- TikTok rewards watch time — front-load the value
- Conversational, authentic tone outperforms polished production
- Use trending sounds and formats for discoverability
- Text overlays are essential (80%+ watch without sound)
- Ask questions to drive comments
- Stitch and duet opportunities increase reach
- Post consistently — algorithm rewards frequency
