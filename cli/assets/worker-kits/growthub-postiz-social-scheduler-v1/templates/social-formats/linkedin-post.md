# LinkedIn Post Format

## Constraints

```yaml
post_max: 3000
hashtag_recommended: 3-5
image_ratio: "1:1 or 1.91:1"
carousel_format: "PDF upload (document post)"
video_max_duration: "10min"
```

## Structure

1. **Hook (first 2 lines)** — Must work before "...see more" truncation (~210 chars). Bold, specific, experience-driven.
2. **Body** — Value, insight, or story. Short paragraphs. One idea per paragraph. White space between sections.
3. **Key takeaway** — Clear lesson or insight (often formatted as a one-liner).
4. **CTA** — Engagement prompt (agree/disagree, share experience, repost).
5. **Hashtags** — 3-5 professional hashtags at end of post.

## Best Practices

- LinkedIn rewards dwell time — longer posts that keep readers scrolling perform well
- Personal stories and lessons outperform corporate announcements
- Use line breaks aggressively — one sentence per line for mobile readability
- Carousel (PDF) posts get highest engagement for educational content
- Polls drive engagement but should be used sparingly
- Tag people and companies only when genuinely relevant
- Post between 8-10am on weekdays for B2B audiences
