# Instagram Carousel Format

## Constraints

```yaml
slides: "2-10"
slide_ratio: "1:1 or 4:5"
caption_max: 2200
hashtag_recommended: 5-15
```

## Structure

### Slide 1 — Cover
- Bold headline that hooks (this is the scroll-stopper)
- Brand-consistent design
- No text overflow

### Slides 2-9 — Content
- One idea per slide
- Large, readable text
- Consistent visual treatment
- Progressive value delivery

### Final Slide — CTA
- Clear action (save, share, follow, link in bio)
- Brand logo or handle

### Caption
- Hook line that complements slide 1
- Brief summary or additional context
- CTA reinforcement
- Hashtag block

## Best Practices

- Carousel posts get 3x more engagement than single images
- Use a visual cliffhanger between slides to encourage swiping
- Keep text large enough to read on mobile (minimum 24pt equivalent)
- Consistent color scheme and typography across all slides
- Educational carousels perform best with 7-10 slides
