## Platform Adaptation Notes

### Instagram
- Max caption length: 2,200 characters
- Line breaks for readability
- Hashtags in caption or first comment
- Hook must work before "...more" truncation (~125 chars)

### LinkedIn
- Max post length: 3,000 characters
- Professional tone per brand persona
- Hashtags at end of post (3-5 max)
- Hook must work before "...see more" truncation (~210 chars)

### TikTok
- Max caption length: 2,200 characters
- Conversational, trend-aware tone
- Hashtags mixed into caption (3-5)
- Hook must match video opening

### Twitter/X
- Max tweet length: 280 characters (or 25,000 for long posts)
- Concise, punchy language
- 1-2 hashtags maximum
- Threads for longer content

### Facebook
- No strict limit but 80-100 chars optimal for engagement
- Conversational tone
- 1-3 hashtags
- Question hooks perform well
