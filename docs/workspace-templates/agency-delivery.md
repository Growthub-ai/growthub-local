# Agency Delivery Template

**Category:** Agency  
**Best for:** Agencies, Delivery leads, Producers  
**Widget count:** 4

## What it includes

| Widget | Kind | Position | Purpose |
|--------|------|----------|---------|
| Delivery Board | view | Left (5×5) | Workstream + owner tracking table |
| Utilization | chart | Center-top (3×4) | Utilization rate chart |
| Client Commitments | rich-text | Right (4×4) | Committed scope, launch date, risks |
| Delivery Portal | iframe | Bottom-left (6×4) | Embed a client-facing delivery portal |

## Use case

Combines a workstream board, utilization trend, commitment notes, and embedded portal into a single delivery-ops surface. Replace the iframe URL with your project management tool or client portal.
